6.1.2 The monthly report shall consist of:

  • Preventive Maintenance Log
    Preventive maintenance includes both condition-monitoring and life-extending tasks which are scheduled at regular intervals. Some tasks must be performed with energized equipment while other shall be performed by shutting down the equipment. Preventive Maintenance shall be performed within periodic time intervals (based on operating or calendar time) or based on measurement result which shows a degradation of the system /equipment performance (Condition Based Maintenance) as per the approved Maintenance Manual stated above in item 6.1.1 for respective sub-systems. The maintenance works which required to shut down the equipment or will disrupt the operation is advisable to perform the maintenance works during Engineering Hours.
  • Corrective Maintenance Log
    Corrective maintenance shall always have highest priority and requires urgent action to carry out repair works or component replacement, to restore the system to a service ready state. Corrective Maintenance will consist of repair, restoration, or replacement of equipment, which will be performed on-site and off-site (to restore failed unit/equipment into working unit/equipment).
  • Observations (Defects) and Rectification
    All observations made by all parties are reported to the Contractor via Project Operational Control Centre (POCC) for necessary corrective maintenance actions.
